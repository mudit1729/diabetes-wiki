## General Use

1. Identify the dominant risk domain: CKD, heart failure, ASCVD, hypoglycemia, obesity, cost/access, pregnancy, or acute illness.
2. Choose drugs for outcome benefit first when CKD, heart failure, or ASCVD is present.
3. Use A1C as one input, not the only input.
4. Check contraindications, eGFR thresholds, drug interactions, affordability, and patient preference.
5. Reassess after 4-12 weeks for tolerability, hypoglycemia, weight, kidney function, and whether the treatment goal is actually being met.
