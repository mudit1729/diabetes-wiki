# Evidence Comparison Tables

These tables are for clinical orientation. They summarize direction and practical strength of evidence, not a patient-specific prescription.

## Major Treatment Classes

| Class | A1C Effect | Weight | MACE | HF Hospitalization | CKD Progression | Hypoglycemia | Practical Notes |
|-------|------------|--------|------|--------------------|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Metformin | Moderate | Neutral / modest loss | Possible legacy benefit, not modern CVOT-grade | Neutral | Dose-limited by eGFR | Low | Low cost; default initial therapy if tolerated and eGFR allows. |
| SGLT2 inhibitor | Modest-moderate; lower at low eGFR | Modest loss | Benefit strongest in selected CVOTs | Strong benefit | Strong benefit | Low alone | Prioritize in HF/CKD; counsel on genital infection, volume, sick-day/perioperative holds. |
| GLP-1 RA | High with semaglutide/liraglutide/dulaglutide | Loss | Strong for selected agents | Mostly neutral | Emerging/agent-specific; FLOW supports semaglutide in CKD | Low alone | Prioritize ASCVD/weight; GI tolerance, cost, and retinopathy history matter. |
| GIP/GLP-1 agonist | Very high | Large loss | CVOT evidence evolving | Not primary reason yet | Kidney signals evolving | Low alone | Strong glycemic/weight option; outcome positioning depends on CVOT/renal data and access. |
| DPP-4 inhibitor | Modest | Neutral | Neutral | Avoid saxagliptin in HF-prone patients | Neutral | Low alone | Useful when simplicity/tolerability matters; less potent. |
| Sulfonylurea | Moderate-high | Gain | No modern outcome advantage | Neutral | No kidney protection | High | Low cost but hypoglycemia and weight gain limit use. |
| Basal insulin | High | Gain | Neutral by itself | Neutral | No kidney protection | High | Best for symptomatic/high glucose or failure of other therapy; titrate carefully. |
| Pioglitazone | Moderate-high | Gain/edema | Possible stroke/MI signal in selected populations | Can worsen HF | Neutral | Low alone | Avoid in symptomatic HF; consider edema/fracture/weight tradeoffs. |

## Landmark Trial Orientation

| Trial / Program | Population | Main Clinical Use |
|-----------------|------------|-------------------|
| UKPDS | Newly diagnosed type 2 diabetes | Early glycemic control, metformin foundation, BP importance. |
| DCCT / EDIC | Type 1 diabetes | Intensive insulin therapy prevents microvascular complications; legacy effect. |
| ACCORD / ADVANCE / VADT | Long-duration/high-risk type 2 diabetes | Individualize targets; avoid harmful over-intensification. |
| SUSTAIN 1-6 | Type 2 diabetes across treatment settings | Semaglutide A1C/weight efficacy and CVOT signal. |
| LEADER / SUSTAIN-6 / REWIND | Type 2 diabetes CVOTs | GLP-1 RA selection for ASCVD risk reduction. |
| EMPA-REG / CANVAS / DECLARE | Type 2 diabetes CVOTs | SGLT2 selection for CV/HF/kidney risk. |
| CREDENCE / DAPA-CKD / EMPA-KIDNEY | CKD populations | SGLT2 kidney protection framework. |
| FLOW | Type 2 diabetes with CKD | Semaglutide kidney and cardiovascular death benefit. |
