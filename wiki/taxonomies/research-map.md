## Domains

### Diagnosis / Classification
Diabetes diagnosis, prediabetes, type 1 vs type 2 vs LADA vs monogenic diabetes, ketosis-prone diabetes, and secondary diabetes. Anchor sources: ADA Standards of Care, WHO classification, monogenic diabetes consensus resources.

### Glycemic Targets
Individualized A1C, glucose, and time-in-range goals. Anchor evidence: DCCT/EDIC, UKPDS, ACCORD, ADVANCE, VADT, ADA Standards, Endocrine Society hypoglycemia guidance.

### Type 2 Diabetes Initial Therapy
Lifestyle, metformin, early combination therapy, and choosing first injectable therapy. Anchor evidence: UKPDS 34, VERIFY, GRADE, ADA Standards, AACE algorithm.

### Cardiorenal-Metabolic Therapy
Selection of SGLT2 inhibitors, GLP-1 receptor agonists, and GIP/GLP-1 therapy for ASCVD, heart failure, CKD, obesity, and high-risk primary prevention. Anchor trials: EMPA-REG OUTCOME, CANVAS, DECLARE-TIMI 58, LEADER, SUSTAIN-6, REWIND, FLOW, SURPASS-CVOT.

### Chronic Kidney Disease
Albuminuria screening, ACEi/ARB, SGLT2 inhibitor foundation, finerenone, GLP-1 RA layering, nephrology referral. Anchor sources: KDIGO 2022 Diabetes in CKD guideline, ADA-KDIGO consensus report, CREDENCE, DAPA-CKD, EMPA-KIDNEY, FLOW.

### Cardiovascular Risk
ASCVD risk reduction, blood pressure, lipids, antiplatelet use, smoking, obesity, heart failure, and peri-procedural medication issues. Anchor sources: ADA Standards, AHA scientific statements, EMPA-REG, LEADER, SUSTAIN-6, REWIND, DECLARE.

### Insulin / Injectable Therapy
Basal insulin initiation, prandial insulin, GLP-1 RA before insulin where appropriate, fixed-ratio combinations, hypoglycemia mitigation. Anchor evidence: SUSTAIN 4/5, GRADE, ORIGIN, DEVOTE, Endocrine Society hypoglycemia guideline.

### Type 1 Diabetes / Technology
Basal-bolus insulin, CGM, automated insulin delivery, time in range, hypoglycemia prevention, sick-day management, DKA prevention. Anchor evidence: DCCT/EDIC, CGM trials, closed-loop/AID trials, Endocrine Society and ADA technology recommendations.

### Complications Screening
Retinopathy, nephropathy, neuropathy, diabetic foot disease, dental disease, MASLD/MASH, sleep apnea, depression, and cognitive impairment. Anchor sources: ADA Standards, KDIGO, ophthalmology and foot-care guidance.

### Acute / Inpatient Diabetes
DKA/HHS, perioperative diabetes, steroids, enteral/parenteral nutrition, inpatient CGM, discharge transitions. Anchor sources: ADA hospital care standards, Endocrine Society inpatient hyperglycemia and hypoglycemia guidance.

### Pregnancy / Reproductive Health
Preconception care, pre-existing diabetes in pregnancy, gestational diabetes, medication safety, postpartum follow-up. Anchor sources: ADA Standards, ACOG/SMFM guidance, international GDM criteria.

### India / South Asia Practice
Phenotype, early-onset type 2 diabetes, lean diabetes, diet patterns, cost/access constraints, insulin affordability, CGM availability, and high out-of-pocket expenditure. Anchor sources should include Indian Council of Medical Research/Indian diabetes society guidance where available, plus global evidence contextualized for local practice.
