# Empagliflozin

## Best Fit

| Use Case | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Type 2 diabetes with established ASCVD | EMPA-REG OUTCOME supports CV and mortality benefit. |
| Heart failure with diabetes | SGLT2 inhibitor class benefit is strong for HF hospitalization reduction. |
| CKD with eligible eGFR/albuminuria profile | Kidney protection is a core reason to consider SGLT2 therapy. |

## Before Starting

- Check eGFR, volume status, blood pressure, genital infection history, DKA risk, low-carb/fasting pattern, upcoming surgery, and insulin deficiency risk.
- Explain sick-day and perioperative holds.
- Review diuretic intensity and orthostasis risk.

## Monitoring

| Item | Practical Check |
|------|-----------------|
| eGFR / creatinine | Expect possible early hemodynamic dip; interpret clinically. |
| Volume / BP | Watch dizziness, dehydration, diuretic overlap. |
| Genital infections | Counsel early reporting and hygiene. |
| Ketoacidosis symptoms | Check ketones when acidosis symptoms occur even if glucose is not very high. |

## Do Not Use / Pause

- Do not use for type 1 diabetes or DKA treatment.
- Hold for major surgery/prolonged fasting, serious illness, dehydration, or suspected ketoacidosis.
- Follow current label and guideline eGFR thresholds for the indication being treated.
