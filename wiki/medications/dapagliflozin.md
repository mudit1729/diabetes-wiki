# Dapagliflozin

## Best Fit

| Use Case | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Type 2 diabetes with heart failure risk | DECLARE-TIMI 58 supports HF hospitalization benefit. |
| CKD with eligible eGFR/albuminuria profile | DAPA-CKD supports kidney outcome benefit. |
| Broad primary prevention risk profile | DECLARE included many patients without established ASCVD. |

## Before Starting

- Check eGFR, volume status, BP, diuretic use, genital infection history, DKA risk, upcoming procedures, and cost.
- Counsel that glucose-lowering effect is weaker at lower eGFR, while cardiorenal reasons may remain.
- Give written sick-day and perioperative hold instructions.

## Monitoring

| Item | Practical Check |
|------|-----------------|
| Kidney function | Baseline and follow-up by CKD/HF risk. |
| Volume status | Especially if on diuretics or low BP. |
| Genital infections | Ask directly; treat early. |
| Ketosis risk | Evaluate nausea, abdominal pain, dyspnea, malaise, fasting, or low-carb intake. |

## Do Not Use / Pause

- Do not use for type 1 diabetes or DKA treatment.
- Hold for major illness, surgery, prolonged fasting, dehydration, or suspected ketoacidosis.
- Follow current label and guideline eGFR thresholds for the indication.
